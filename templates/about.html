About Smart Traffic Management System

Our system leverages cutting-edge AI to predict and manage urban traffic, making cities smarter and more efficient.

Project Description

The Smart Traffic Management System is an innovative solution designed to tackle urban traffic congestion. By integrating advanced AI algorithms, real-time data analysis, and predictive modeling, our system optimizes traffic flow, reduces commute times, and enhances road safety. This project is part of the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025, showcasing our commitment to developing impactful solutions for real-world challenges.

Our Goal

Our primary goal is to transform urban traffic management, making it smoother, safer, and smarter. By providing predictive analytics and live dashboards, we empower city planners and commuters to make informed decisions, leading to reduced congestion, improved air quality, and enhanced overall quality of life.

Project Details

Our system is built on a robust technical foundation, utilizing the Prophet model for accurate traffic prediction, a Flask backend for seamless data processing, Leaflet for interactive mapping, and Chart.js for dynamic data visualization. This combination of technologies ensures a reliable, scalable, and user-friendly experience.
